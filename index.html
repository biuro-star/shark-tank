Math section — Sharks can model this. Be precise, not emotional.

The Three Core Insights

These drive the entire pitch. Memorize the logic. Deliver in your own words.

1. Foreign Market Mastery

"I built a $1 million business in Poland — a country I had never lived in, in a language I didn't speak. If I cracked Poland from zero — what makes you think I won't crack America?"
  • Proves adaptability — you've done this once already
  • Defuses "Polish founder in America" objection
  • Shows operator humility + execution
  • No other Shark Tank applicant has this profile

2. The Replication Logic

"Poland $1M, 38M people. USA 340M — 8x bigger. Same product, same playbook. The barrier is TRIAL, not awareness. Once they try it — they buy."
  • Concrete math — Sharks can model this
  • Reframes the problem: TRIAL not awareness
  • Justifies use of capital — clear cause-effect
  • 33% word-of-mouth proves trial = conversion

3. Smart Money, Not Just Money

"10 years, never taken outside investment. I don't need your money — I have my own. I need YOU. American mindset. American network. American doors."
  • Rarest Shark Tank pitch type — operator royalty
  • Bootstrapped to $2M = mature business
  • Aligns with what Sharks actually offer
  • Differentiates from 99% of applicants
