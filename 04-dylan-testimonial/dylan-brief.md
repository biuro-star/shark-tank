# 🎤 Dylan Testimonial — Director Plan

> 15-second customer testimonial in Section 3 of the video.  
> Dylan owns 10Ten Fitness Sarasota — and is a real BenchK customer.

---

## 📋 The 3 Questions (ask in this order)

1. **"Why did you put BenchK in your studio?"**
2. **"How often do you use this wall?"**
3. **"Would you recommend BenchK to other gym owners?"** *(optional — only if Dylan is in flow)*

---

## 🎬 Vadym's Exact Lines

**Setup line (turn slightly toward Dylan, smile gently):**

> *"Sharks — meet Dylan. He owns this studio. Dylan..."*
>
> *(Pause. Look at Dylan.)*
>
> *"...why did you put BenchK in your studio?"*

**[Dylan answers — DO NOT INTERRUPT, even if he pauses 2 seconds searching for words]**

> *"Dylan — how often do you use this wall?"*

**[Dylan answers]**

**Closing line (turn back to camera):**

> *"Thanks, brother."*
>
> *(Tap Dylan on shoulder. Dylan exits frame. Vadym returns to main camera.)*

---

## ⛔ What Vadym MUST NOT Do

- ❌ **Do not interrupt Dylan** — even if he pauses searching for a word
- ❌ **Do not finish his sentences** — even if you know what he's about to say
- ❌ **Do not correct him** if he says something slightly wrong (real > perfect)
- ❌ **Do not look at camera** while Dylan talks — look at HIM with attention
- ❌ **Do not say "great answer"** between questions — sounds scripted
- ❌ **Do not script Dylan beforehand** — bullet points only
- ❌ **Do not stand on his right side** — Dylan should be camera-right, so when Vadym turns to him, both faces visible

---


## 🎥 Camera Setup for Dylan Moment

- **Shot:** Medium two-shot — both Vadym and Dylan in frame, BenchK visible behind
- **Position:** Vadym camera-left, Dylan camera-right (so Vadym turns naturally toward camera)
- **Distance:** Both standing, casual, about 1 arm's length apart
- **Hand position:** Vadym's hand can be loosely on BenchK rung
- **Eye contact during questions:** Vadym looks at Dylan (NOT camera)

---

## 🎙️ AUDIO CRITICAL

> **If only ONE lavalier microphone is available — give it to DYLAN, not Vadym.**

Reason: Vadym's voice is recorded across the rest of the video. Dylan's voice ONLY exists in this 15 seconds — it must be crystal clear.

**Best setup:** TWO lavalier microphones (one on each), recording on separate audio channels.

---

## 🎬 Shoot 3 Takes

| Take | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Take 1** | Standard pace, all 3 questions |
| **Take 2** | Faster, only Question 1 + Question 3 |
| **Take 3** | Most casual, ask whichever Dylan answered best in 1-2 |

Editor will select best moments from all 3 takes.

---

## 🔄 BACKUP PLAN — if Dylan freezes on camera

> *(Rare for natural speakers, but happens)*

**Fallback line (Vadym says alone, no Dylan on camera):**

> *"Behind me — 10Ten Fitness Sarasota. Owned by Dylan. Real American customer."*

This way:
- ✅ Visual location preserved
- ✅ Customer credibility partly preserved
- ✅ No awkward frozen moment in final video
- ⚠️ Lose some emotional impact

**Always have plan B.**
