# 🚨 DO NOT SAY ON CAMERA

> Sharks vetting team will catch any of these. Each one can kill the deal.

---

## ❌ Identity / Politics

| ❌ NEVER SAY | ✅ INSTEAD SAY | Reason |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| "I'm Ukrainian" (alone) | "I'm Polish, with Ukrainian origins" | Loses 10 years of Polish achievement |
| "I'm Polish" (alone) | "I'm Polish, with Ukrainian origins" | Denies your real heritage |
| "I escaped from Ukraine" | "My family moved to Poland in 2015" | Refugee framing wrong for E2 investor |
| "Russia invaded my country" | (don't bring up war at all) | Politically charged, brand risk |
| "I'm Russian-speaking" | (just speak English) | Politically problematic in 2026 |
| Putin / Russia / war references | (none) | Off-brand for fitness business |

---

## ❌ Visa / Immigration

| ❌ NEVER SAY | ✅ INSTEAD SAY | Reason |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| "I immigrated to America" | "I came here on an investor visa" | E2 is non-immigrant visa — legal risk |
| "I moved here permanently" | "I'm developing BenchK in the US market" | Same as above |
| "I'm settling in Florida" | "We have our warehouse in Florida" | Same as above |
| "I'm a refugee" | (no — see above) | Wrong visa class entirely |

---

## ❌ Business Overclaims

| ❌ NEVER SAY | ✅ INSTEAD SAY | Reason |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| "Sold in 30 countries" | "Strong distribution in 5 EU countries + online across Europe" | Vetting team will challenge "30" |
| "Made in Poland and the United States" | "Made in Poland — US production planned" | US production not started yet (catalog wording aspirational) |
| "Patented technology" (about BenchK ladder) | "Award-winning design" | BenchK has no patent on the ladder itself |
| "Recoil's patent is ours" | "We distribute Recoil — patented system by our partner" | Recoil is Robert Bowles' IP, not BenchK's |
| "100 million in revenue" | "$2 million in global revenue" | Stick to verified |
| "We're profitable since day 1" | "We've been profitable for years, no outside investors" | Unverifiable — see ERP records |

---

## ❌ Numbers You Haven't Verified

> **Rule: If you don't have it on paper, don't say it on camera.**

If a Shark asks about something not in [memorize-cold.md](memorize-cold.md), say:

> *"I'd need to verify that exact number — I'd rather give you the accurate figure than guess."*

This shows operator discipline. Sharks **respect** founders who admit limits.

## ❌ Emotional Pitfalls

| ❌ AVOID | Why |
|---------|-----|
| "Please give me a chance" | Begging energy — Sharks read it instantly |
| "I really really need this" | Desperate — kills deal |
| "I'll do anything for an offer" | Same as above |
| "We're struggling in the US" | Negative — replace with facts |
| Crying / breaking down | Mindy is OK with emotion — but not breakdown |

## ❌ Wardrobe / Set Mistakes

- ❌ White shirt (washes out on camera)
- ❌ Busy patterns (distracting on TV)
- ❌ Visible BenchK logo on clothing (looks like an ad)
- ❌ Watches catching light (reflects camera)
- ❌ Unkempt hair / beard (unprofessional)
- ❌ Phone visible / vibrating during recording
- ❌ Coffee cup or water bottle on set
- ❌ Heavy meal before recording (kills energy)
- ❌ Alcohol night before (red eyes, slow speech)

## ❌ Production Mistakes

- ❌ Vertical (portrait) video orientation — ABC requires HORIZONTAL
- ❌ Handheld camera shake — ABC requires tripod
- ❌ Background music — ABC requires NO music
